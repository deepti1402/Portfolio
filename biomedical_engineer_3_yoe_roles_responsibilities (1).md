## 1. Medical Equipment Management

### Preventive Maintenance (PM)
- Plan, schedule, and execute **preventive maintenance** for critical and non-critical medical equipment.
- Follow manufacturer-recommended PM schedules and hospital SOPs.
- Perform functional checks, performance verification, and safety testing.
- Maintain PM checklists, service tags, and equipment status labels.

### Corrective Maintenance & Troubleshooting
- Diagnose and rectify equipment breakdowns in ICU, OT, NICU, wards, OPD, and diagnostics.
- Perform first-level and second-level troubleshooting of biomedical devices.
- Escalate unresolved issues to OEMs/vendors with clear fault analysis.
- Ensure rapid response to minimize downtime and clinical disruption.

### Calibration & Performance Verification
- Conduct routine **calibration** of measuring and monitoring equipment.
- Maintain traceable calibration records as per NABH requirements.
- Coordinate with external calibration agencies when required.
- Ensure calibration stickers and certificates are updated and audit-ready.

## 3. Regulatory & Quality Compliance

### NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals)
- Maintain equipment inventory with unique identification numbers.
- Ensure PM, calibration, and breakdown records are complete and updated.
- Support internal and external audits.
- Participate in daily biomedical rounds and quality checks.
- Assist in NABH 5th Edition documentation and implementation.

### AERB (Atomic Energy Regulatory Board)
- Maintain AERB documentation for radiation-based equipment.
- Assist in regulatory approvals, renewals, and inspections.
- Ensure radiation safety signage, TLD badges coordination, and safety protocols.

### PNDT Act Compliance
- Maintain PNDT records for ultrasound equipment.
- Ensure proper display of statutory notices.
- Coordinate with radiology staff for compliance checks.
- Support inspections and audits related to PNDT.
